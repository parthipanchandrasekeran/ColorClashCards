## 2. What Data We Collect

We collect only the data necessary to provide multiplayer gaming features. Here is exactly what we collect:

### Account Information

| Data | When Collected | Required? |
|------|----------------|-----------|
| Display name | When you sign in with Google | Yes (for multiplayer) |
| Profile photo URL | When you sign in with Google | No (optional display) |
| Unique user ID | Automatically generated at sign-in | Yes (identifies your account) |
| Guest account flag | When you play as guest | Yes (to distinguish account types) |
| Account creation date | When you first sign in | Yes (account management) |

### Game Data

| Data | When Collected | Purpose |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Room membership | When you create or join a game room | To connect you with other players |
| Ready status | When you mark yourself ready in a lobby | To coordinate game start |
| Card hands | During Color Clash matches | Your private cards (only you can see them) |
| Game actions | When you play cards, draw, or roll dice | To sync gameplay across players |
| Token positions | During Ludo matches | To track game progress |
| Move history | During Ludo matches | To validate and display moves |

### Connection Data

| Data | When Collected | Purpose |
|------|----------------|---------|
| Online status | While in a multiplayer game | To show other players you are connected |
| Last active time | Periodically during games | To detect disconnections |
| Disconnect time | When you lose connection | To allow reconnection within a time window |

### Device Preferences

| Data | Where Stored | Purpose |
|------|--------------|---------|
| Dark mode setting | On your device only | To remember your display preference |

### What We Do NOT Collect

- Email addresses
- Phone numbers
- Precise location
- Payment information
- Contacts or address book
- Photos from your device
- Advertising identifiers
- Usage analytics or behavior tracking

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the game experience:

- **Display name and photo**: Shown to other players so they know who they are playing with
- **User ID**: Links your account to your game progress and active sessions
- **Game state data**: Enables real-time multiplayer gameplay
- **Connection status**: Lets other players know if you are connected or temporarily disconnected
- **Dark mode preference**: Applies your chosen visual theme

We do not:
- Sell your data to anyone
- Use your data for advertising
- Build profiles about you
- Track your behavior across apps
- Send you marketing communications

## 4. Data Sharing

### Google and Firebase Services

We use Google's Firebase platform to provide core app functionality:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Firebase Authentication | Sign-in and account management | Google ID token (during sign-in only) |
| Cloud Firestore | Store game rooms and match data | All game data listed in Section 2 |

Firebase is provided by Google LLC. When you use our app, your data is processed according to:
- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Firebase Terms of Service](https://firebase.google.com/terms)

Firebase servers are located in the United States. If you are outside the US, your data will be transferred to US servers.

### No Other Sharing

We do not share your data with:
- Advertisers
- Analytics companies
- Data brokers
- Social media platforms
- Any other third parties

## 5. Data Retention

| Data Type | How Long We Keep It |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Account information | Until you delete your account |
| Game room data | Until the room is deleted or expires (inactive rooms are cleaned up periodically) |
| Match data (cards, moves, actions) | Until the match ends and room is deleted |
| Connection/presence data | Until you leave the game room |
| Dark mode preference | Until you uninstall the app or clear app data |

### Automatic Cleanup

- Game rooms that have ended are eligible for deletion
- Temporary game data (hands, actions, presence) is removed when matches conclude
- Guest accounts may be cleaned up after extended inactivity

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption in transit**: All data sent between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- **Access controls**: Firestore security rules ensure:
  - Only you can read your private card hand
  - Only room members can see room data
  - Only the room host can modify room settings
- **Authentication**: Google Sign-In provides secure account access
- **No passwords stored**: We never see or store your Google password

While we implement these protections, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

## 8. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### Access Your Data

You can see your display name and profile photo in the app. Your game history is visible while rooms remain active.

### Delete Your Data

You can delete your data in the following ways:

| What to Delete | How to Delete |
|----------------|---------------|
| Leave a game room | Tap the back button and confirm leaving |
| Your account and all data | Contact us at the email above with your request |
| Local preferences | Clear app data in your device settings, or uninstall the app |

### Account Deletion Request

To request complete deletion of your account and associated data:

1. Email us at the contact address above
2. Include "Account Deletion Request" in the subject line
3. Provide the display name associated with your account
4. We will process your request within 30 days

### Guest Accounts

If you played as a guest, your data is linked to your device. Uninstalling the app or clearing app data will remove your local access. Contact us if you need server-side guest data removed.
