## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Get ready for Color Clash Cards - the ultimate card matching showdown!

Match cards by color or number to empty your hand before your opponents. Play special action cards to skip, reverse, or force opponents to draw. Use wild cards strategically to change the game's direction. Don't forget to call "Last Card" when you're down to one - or face the penalty!

GAME MODES

Solo vs Bots
Challenge 1-3 AI opponents with adjustable difficulty levels:
- Easy: Perfect for learning the rules
- Normal: A fair challenge for casual play
- Hard: Test your skills against cunning AI

Online Multiplayer
- Create private rooms and share codes with friends
- Browse public rooms to join games worldwide
- Real-time gameplay with smooth synchronization
- Fill empty seats with bots (host choice)

GAMEPLAY FEATURES

- Classic color and number matching rules
- Action cards: Skip, Reverse, Draw Two
- Wild cards: Choose any color or force +4 draw
- "Last Card" call system with penalties
- Smart card grouping by color for easy selection
- Intuitive touch controls with smooth animations

SOCIAL FEATURES

- Google Sign-In for persistent stats
- Play as guest without an account
- Private rooms with shareable codes
- Global leaderboards (coming soon)

DESIGNED FOR EVERYONE

- Clean Material Design 3 interface
- Dark mode support
- Works on phones and tablets
- No ads in gameplay

Download now and start clashing!
