Account & Data Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

Option 1: Email Request

Send an email to: parthipan_chandrasekaran@yahoo.com

Please include the email address associated with your Google account so we can locate your data.

— OR —

Option 2: In-App Deletion

  1. Open Color Clash Cards
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Go to Settings
  4. Tap "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm deletion

Data That Will Be Deleted

  • Your user profile (UID, display name, profile photo URL)
  • Account creation timestamp
  • Any active game room memberships
  • Game history (card hands, moves, actions)
  • Connection/presence data

Data Retention

All data is permanently deleted within 30 days of request.

Once deletion is complete, this action cannot be undone. You will need to create a new account if you wish to use the app again.

Guest Users

Guest accounts use an anonymous Firebase identifier. Uninstalling the app removes local access to the account.

To delete server-side guest data, email us with details about when you played (approximate dates) so we can locate your data.
