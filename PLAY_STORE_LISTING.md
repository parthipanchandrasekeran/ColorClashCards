## Full Description (4000 characters max)

Color Clash Cards is a fast-paced, family-friendly card game where you match colors and numbers to be the first to empty your hand!

EASY TO LEARN, FUN TO MASTER
Match cards by color or number, use special action cards strategically, and don't forget to call "Last Card" when you're down to one!

MULTIPLE WAYS TO PLAY

Play vs Computer
- Challenge 1-3 AI opponents
- Three difficulty levels: Easy, Normal, and Hard
- Perfect for practice or quick games on the go
- No internet required!

Play Online with Friends
- Create a private room and share the code
- Join friends' rooms instantly
- Real-time multiplayer with smooth synchronization
- Automatic reconnection if you get disconnected

Guest Mode
- Jump right into the action without signing up
- Play offline against bots immediately
- Upgrade to a Google account anytime to unlock online play

GAME FEATURES

Classic Gameplay
- Match by color or number
- 108-card deck with four vibrant colors
- Strategic depth with action cards

Special Cards
- Skip: Make the next player lose their turn
- Reverse: Change the direction of play
- Draw Two: Force the next player to draw cards
- Wild: Change the color to anything you want
- Wild Draw Four: The ultimate power move!

Smart AI Opponents
- Three difficulty levels to match your skill
- Bots make strategic decisions, not random moves
- Great for learning or warming up before online play

ONLINE MULTIPLAYER

- Sign in with Google for secure online play
- Create rooms with unique, shareable codes
- Browse public rooms to join random games
- Host controls when the game starts
- Bots automatically fill empty seats
- Seamless reconnection to games in progress

BEAUTIFUL DESIGN

- Modern Material Design 3 interface
- Original card artwork with sleek diagonal stripes
- Felt-style game table background
- Smooth animations for every action
- Clear turn indicators so you always know what's happening
- Dark mode support for comfortable night play

PRIVACY FIRST

- No ads ever
- Minimal data collection
- Play offline without any account
- Your data is never sold or shared
- Request data deletion anytime

PERFECT FOR

- Family game nights
- Quick games during breaks
- Playing with friends across distances
- Card game enthusiasts
- Anyone looking for casual fun!
