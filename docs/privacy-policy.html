Privacy Policy for Color Clash Cards

Last Updated: January 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Color Clash Cards ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

Google Sign-In (Optional)
When you choose to sign in with Google, we receive:

  • Your display name
  • Your email address
  • Your Google profile photo URL

This information is used to identify you in multiplayer games and maintain your game statistics.

Guest Mode
If you play as a guest, we assign you a random anonymous identifier. No personal information is collected.

Information Collected Automatically

Gameplay Data

  • Game actions (cards played, matches won/lost)
  • Room participation for multiplayer games
  • Anonymous usage statistics

This data is used solely to operate the game and synchronize multiplayer sessions.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Authenticate your identity in multiplayer games
  • Display your name and avatar to other players
  • Synchronize real-time gameplay across devices
  • Track your game statistics and match history
  • Improve the app's functionality

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Firebase services provided by Google:

  • Firebase Authentication: Manages user identity
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores game data and statistics

All data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL). We do not store passwords; authentication is handled entirely by Google.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

Firebase (Google)

  • Purpose: Authentication, database, analytics
  • Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Play Services

  • Purpose: Google Sign-In
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent

Children's Privacy

Color Clash Cards is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your data in the app's settings
  • Delete: Remove your account and all associated data
  • Opt-out: Play as a guest without providing personal information

For account and data deletion, please see our Account Deletion page.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last Updated" date.
