Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Summary: Color Clash Cards collects your display name and profile photo (from Google Sign-In) to show other players who you are. Game data like room membership, card hands, and moves are stored temporarily to enable multiplayer. We use Firebase by Google to securely store this data. We do not collect analytics, show ads, or sell your information. You can delete your data anytime by leaving games or requesting account deletion.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide the game experience:

  • Display name and photo: Shown to other players so they know who they are playing with
  • User ID: Links your account to your game progress and active sessions
  • Game state data: Enables real-time multiplayer gameplay
  • Connection status: Lets other players know if you are connected or temporarily disconnected
  • Dark mode preference: Applies your chosen visual theme

We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Build profiles about you
  • Track your behavior across apps
  • Send you marketing communications

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption in transit: All data sent between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Access controls: Firestore security rules ensure only you can read your private card hand, only room members can see room data, and only the room host can modify room settings
  • Authentication: Google Sign-In provides secure account access
  • No passwords stored: We never see or store your Google password

7. Children's Privacy

Color Clash Cards is intended for a general audience and does not specifically target children.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • If you are a parent and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us
  • Upon verification, we will delete such information promptly
